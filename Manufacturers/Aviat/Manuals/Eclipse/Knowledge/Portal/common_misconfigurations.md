# Common Misconfigurations (Critical for ARIA)

## 1. Protection Set After Link Config

Result:
- Incorrect synchronization
- Link instability

---

## 2. Incorrect Modulation Selection

Result:
- Poor performance OR link failure

---

## 3. Tx Power Too Low

Result:
- No ATPC recovery possible

---

## 4. Ignoring SNR

Result:
- Good signal but high BER

---

## 5. Using ATPC in High Interference

Result:
- Ineffective power adjustments :contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}

---

## 6. Plug-in / Hardware Mismatch

Result:
- System errors or non-functional link

---

## 7. Forgetting Tx Mute

Result:
- No transmission (common startup issue)
