# Protection Modes

## Link-Level Protection

Types:
- 1+0 → No protection
- 1+1 → Hot standby
- Ring protection
- XPIC (co-channel dual link)

---

## Ethernet Protection Methods

1. Wireless protection (radio level)
2. RSTP (network switching)
3. Link aggregation
4. Parallel-path operation :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

---

## Link Aggregation Logic

- Multiple links share traffic
- If one fails:
  → traffic shifts to remaining links :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}

---

## XPIC

- Two radios share same frequency
- Doubles capacity using polarization

---

## Critical Behavior

If bandwidth insufficient:
